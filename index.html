Important Dates

Income Tax and Capital Gains Tax

  • Income Tax Form 11 due date - 31st October 2020, or 10th December 2020 if filing online
  • Capital Gains Tax payment date - 15th December 2020 for gains made up to 30th November 2020, or 31st December 2021 for gains made up to 31st December 2020

Corporation Tax

  • CT1 return and payment due - 23rd of the 9th month after the end of the company accounting period
  • E.G. Year end 31st December 2019 will be due 23rd September 2020

VAT

  • Bi-Monthly filers - return and payment due 23rd of the month after the end of the period
  • Quarterly filers - return and payment due 23rd of the month after the end of the quarter
  • Annual filers - return and payment due 23rd Jnauary the following year
